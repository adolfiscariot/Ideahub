Your space to share actionable improvements

This page is your space for practical, actionable ideas that can improve productivity, efficiency, or working conditions. Focus on realistic ideas that can be implemented in weeks or months.

Valid Ideas Look Like

Automating Attendance Registration

Use fingerprints to register attendance instead of manually writing it on Odoo.

Knowledge Sharing Space

A space e.g. excel sheet for people to post articles/posts/blogs they read that others might find interesting.

Ideas to Avoid

We should explore AI more

Too broad — what specific problem would it solve?

We should build an app to sell second hand cars

Out of scope — focus on specific, tangible changes within the workplace

Tip: Ask yourself, “Is this idea work related, will it improve productivity or make work more efficient?" If yes, it belongs here. If no, it has to go.
