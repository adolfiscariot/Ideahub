💡 Contributing Ideas

  • To contribute ideas you must be a member of a group. You can either join an existing group or create a group of your own that best fits the category your idea falls under.
  • Once an idea is promoted, it cannot be deleted.

👥 Joining Groups

  • Public Groups
    Click the join group button to Join instantly and you'll be redirected to the group's Ideas page automatically.
  • Private Groups
    Your request will be pending until an admin approves it.

🚪 Leaving Groups

  • If You're an Admin
    You must transfer ownership to another member before you can leave.
📝
Note: Transferring ownership automatically removes you from the group. Ownership can be given to any current member of the group.

🔔 Notifications

  • Admins receive alerts for pending join requests via the notification bell or the View Requests button on Ideas page.
