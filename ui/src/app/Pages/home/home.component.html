👋 Welcome to IdeaHub

IdeaHub is Adept Technologies’ internal platform for capturing, tracking, and turning employee ideas into real, executable initiatives.

📊 Getting Started

  • The homepage gives you a quick snapshot of idea activity at Adept and your personal involvement.
  • To participate, go to the Groups page and join an existing group or create a new one to contribute valuable ideas.
  • When an idea gains enough support and meets the required threshold, it is promoted into a project. At that point, an overseer is assigned to act as the project owner, and the idea moves from discussion into execution within the company.
